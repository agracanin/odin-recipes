Perfect Homemade Lasasgna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of wide flat pasta noodles, tomato-based meat sauce, and creamy cheese. The dish is typically baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and the pasta is tender. Lasagna is a hearty and satisfying dish that is perfect for feeding a crowd or for meal prep.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium-high heat until no longer pink. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet, and stir to combine. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes until it thickens.
  5. In a medium bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups of the shredded mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and egg until well combined.
  6. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, followed by another layer of meat sauce, and then a layer of the cheese mixture. Repeat these layers two more times, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the lasagna.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
